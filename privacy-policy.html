Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Welcome to American Home Recipes ("we", "our", "us"). We highly respect the privacy of our readers and home chefs. This policy documents the types of data we might capture, how we utilise cookies, and how we deliver advertisements, particularly via the Google AdSense network.

1. Personal Information We Collect

We are a static information-first website. We only collect personal information when you explicitly complete details on our web forms. This covers:

  • Newsletter Subscriptions: If you join our table club email list, we store your email address safely to send recipe letters.
  • Contact Forms: If you message our chefs, we capture your name, email, subject choice, and the text body content to compose an educational response.

2. Logging and Analytics Data

Similar to standard, high-quality, fast-loading web servers, our host automatically captures standard logging rows: browser user-agents, IP addresses, internet service providers (ISPs), entry/leaving pages, navigation timestamps, and country origins. This data is fully anonymous and used solely to run performance checks, secure systems, and optimize page speeds.

3. Cookies and Advertising Partners (Google AdSense)

We work with third-party advertising services, particularly Google AdSense, to display custom sponsor banners supporting our preservation activities. These partners use tracking cookies to serve relevant advertisements:

  • Google and third-party vendors use cookies (e.g., the DoubleClick cookie) to serve relevant ads based on users' visits to our site or other portals on the web.
  • Users may easily opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings at any time.

4. Data Protection Standards

We do not lease, trade, or transfer your contact details to third-party marketing services or commercial databases. Your email is only used internally to deliver recipe news. We use standard browser protections to prevent data intercept.

5. Your Legal Rights

Under global, safe browsing guidelines (GDPR, CCPA), you are fully entitled to request access to the information we hold about you, or ask to delete your email address from our subscriber tables. To proceed, please fill in your credentials on our Contact Page.
